# Exploratory Data Analysis Report

## Summary
The analysis focused on understanding sales performance, customer behavior, and profitability trends.

## Key Findings
- Consumer segment dominates total sales contribution.
- Sales show seasonal patterns across months.
- States such as California and Florida contribute higher revenue.
- Discount levels have a negative impact on profit.
- Average Order Value provides insight into order-level revenue.

## Recommendation
Optimize discount strategies to improve profitability while maintaining sales volume.
